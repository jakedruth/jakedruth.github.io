About

Hello. I make and play video games and sometimes I create websites.

Game Programmer & Web Developer

Based out of New England, I received my bachelor's degree in Game Programming at Champlain College and my associate's degree in Animation and Game Programming at NHTI's Concord's Community College.

  • Degree: Bachelor's
  • City: Burlington, VT, USA
  • Email: jakedruth@gmail.com

As you have probably gathered, I am a video game programmer with interests and skills in web development. My love for making games began as a kid, playing games was not enough for me. I always wanted to learn how they worked. Growing up I always dabbled with programming but it wasn't until my first class in college when I knew I wanted to make games professionally. Working on games provides a difficult challenge. It creates a high reward of satisfaction just to see my creations on a screen, when that one bug gets fixed, or even just figuring out how to implement a new algorithm.

I am predominantly a C# developer as I prefer to work in Unity. However, I have skills with other various languages and engines, such as Unreal and Android Studios. I have made several applications for Windows, Linux, Android, and the Web.
